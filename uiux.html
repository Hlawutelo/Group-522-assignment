What is UX?

By Admin · April 2025

User Experience (UX) refers to the overall experience a person has when interacting with a product or service, especially in terms of how easy or pleasing it is to use. Good UX design focuses on understanding users, their needs, values, abilities, and limitations.

Principles of Good UX Design

By Admin · April 2025
  • User-Centric: Design with the user's needs and goals in mind.
  • Usability: Ensure the product is easy to use and intuitive.
  • Accessibility: Make the product usable for people of all abilities.
  • Consistency: Maintain uniformity in design elements and interactions.
  • Feedback: Provide clear feedback to users about their actions.
